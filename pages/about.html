Our Story

Our website was created in order for our vistors to find a book using quotes they have an interest in, rather than through judging by the cover of the book. This allows for customers to buy books they are content with reading and allows readers to be matched with books they enjoy. Our website categorises the quotes into genres, whatever genre your interested in that day you can pick a quote and find a book that suits you. Moreover, sometimes you may have a naggy quote in your head but are unable to find what book its from our website will also allow you to be matched with that book!
